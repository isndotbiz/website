Investor Summary

AuraLLM occupies a unique position in the $7B+ wellness app market by combining voice-derived energy analysis, structured habit tracking, and an open AR glasses schema into a single privacy-first experience. Unlike fitness trackers that focus on physical metrics or meditation apps limited to guided sessions, AuraLLM reflects how users feel and how they show up through a dual-ring energy dial that separates internal state from outward impact.

The core pipeline runs entirely on-device. Voice samples are processed in-memory and immediately discarded — only numeric scores persist. Habit entries stay in the local Room database. This architecture means the product delivers full value at zero marginal cost per user while the freemium model ($4.99/month Aura+) gates advanced analytics, custom analysis windows, and AR visualization without restricting core privacy guarantees.

Early integration with this form factor builds brand association and switching costs before the market matures.

Deep Dive: The Dual-Ring Energy Dial

At the heart of AuraLLM is a concentric visualization that separates how you feel from how you show up. Two rings, one aura score — a reflective lens, not a clinical verdict.

Two Dimensions of Energy

The outer ring captures Self Energy — how you feel internally, derived from self-report or voice analysis. The inner ring captures Others Impact — how you show up to the world, calculated from self-energy plus a habit bonus that rewards consistent positive actions. The combined score maps to a living aura state: Radiant (70-100, violet), Steady (45-69, teal), Recharging (20-44, amber), or Red Zone (0-19, rose). Each state pulses a DMT-palette colour through the dial and optional glasses HUD.

Chakra Guidance Ladder

Energy maps to a six-level chakra ladder from Root (0-24, grounding) to Crown (85+, purpose and stillness). Each level delivers three specific micro-interventions — "Place a hand on your chest and breathe in 4, out 6" at Heart level, or "Take a slow grounding walk or stand barefoot" at Root. The system applies a self-centred penalty when Self Energy exceeds Others Impact by 20+ points and a red zone penalty, rewarding outward awareness over personal highs. This structured framework turns abstract energy scores into actionable next steps.

Voice Energy Extraction

With explicit, revocable consent managed through SpeechConsentManager backed by DataStore, AuraLLM captures a brief voice sample and runs SpeechFeatureExtractor entirely in-memory. Raw audio is processed and immediately discarded — only three numeric scores persist: speaking rate (words per minute), tone warmth (0-100), and vocal energy (0-100). Future releases will integrate a TFLite prosody model for richer feature extraction without any change to the privacy guarantees. The consent flow is binary and reversible: users tap "Enable Voice" to grant, "Revoke" to disable, with no dark patterns or friction.

Habit Bonus Algorithm

Six habit categories (Meal, Sleep, Exercise, Hydration, Meditation, Social) accept daily log entries rated 1-5 for quality. A rolling 3-day average drives the Habit Bonus: excellent habits (4-5 avg) add +10 to +15 points to Others Impact, good habits (3-4 avg) add +5 to +10, and building habits (1-3 avg) add +0 to +5. This transparent, compounding system ensures that consistent positive actions visibly improve the energy portrait over days, creating a natural engagement loop grounded in genuine behavioural feedback rather than gamification tricks.

Platform Architecture

🎤

Voice Engine

On-Device Speech Analysis

SpeechFeatureExtractor processes audio in-memory with zero disk writes. Consent-gated via DataStore, the pipeline extracts speaking rate, tone warmth, and vocal energy as numeric scores. Raw audio is discarded immediately after extraction. Future TFLite integration will add prosody analysis while maintaining the same privacy guarantees and consent model.

📈

Habit Engine

Structured Wellness Tracking

Room database stores HabitEntry records across six categories with 1-5 quality ratings. A 3-day rolling average drives the Habit Bonus algorithm that transparently influences Others Impact scores. Entries never leave the device. The compounding effect — consistent good habits visibly improving energy scores — creates natural retention without artificial streaks.

🤖

LLM Integration

Pluggable Insight Generation

The LlmProvider interface accepts energy readings and habit summaries, returning reflective narrative insights. Currently backed by a deterministic template engine (zero cost, zero network), with planned on-device TFLite and GGUF providers for maximum privacy, plus optional cloud API providers for deepest narratives. All insights append "(For wellness reflection only.)"

👓

AR Glasses

Open DialState Schema

The DialState is a serializable JSON object containing selfEnergy, othersImpact, auraColorHex, label, chakra level with suggestions, and timestamp. The in-app preview simulates the glasses HUD with pulsing aura ring, energy score, and chakra bar. Camera passthrough shows the overlay on a live feed. BLE broadcast and companion SDK are planned for hardware pairing.

🔒

Privacy-First

Architectural Guarantee

Local-first Room database. No raw audio on disk or network. SpeechConsentManager via DataStore with explicit grant/revoke. No analytics SDK, no advertising profiles, no third-party data brokers. The entire core pipeline — scoring, dial, habits, speech — works fully offline. Red Zone is self-report only; the app never claims to detect distress or diagnose conditions.

🕐

Background Analysis

WorkManager Scheduling

AnalysisWorker runs every six hours via WorkManager, consuming a 3-day rolling window of EnergyReading and HabitEntry data. The worker feeds aggregated scores to LlmProvider.generateInsight(), writes the reflective insight back to the reading, and surfaces it in the UI. Battery-aware scheduling respects Doze mode and App Standby restrictions.
