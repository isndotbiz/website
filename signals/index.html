How this works

1. Twelve sources

SEC EDGAR (8-K + SC 13D + Form 4), Senate & House STOCK Act PTRs, Polymarket & Kalshi, EU GDPR enforcement, USDA agricultural basis, Federal Register, ClinicalTrials.gov, CISA KEV, USPTO PTAB, EMMA muni bonds, SAM.gov contracts, CNIPA patents.

2. Cross-signal fusion

Single-source alerts are commodity. The edge is in the overlaps — congressional trade plus EDGAR filing in the same name, federal rule plus sector-correlated 8-K. Our correlator surfaces those automatically.

3. Forward returns auto-backfilled

Every alert writes one row to a JSONL ledger at the moment it fires. A daily backfill job fills in the 5-day, 20-day, and 60-day stock returns once the windows close. We don't get to revise predictions after the fact.

4. Public feed delays the edge

The live subscriber feed is real-time. The public ledger is intentionally delayed (7 days at launch, ratcheting to 30 once the dataset matures) so you can audit the methodology without consuming the edge. Source-of-truth timestamps mean every claim is verifiable.
