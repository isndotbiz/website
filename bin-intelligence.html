Payment Security

Payment Fraud Intelligence Platform

Real-time BIN enrichment and fraud analytics engine serving the $32B payment fraud prevention market — combining automated feed ingestion, Neutrino API enrichment, and live risk scoring dashboards for e-commerce transaction security.

Investor Summary

Fraud prevention teams need reliable BIN intelligence to evaluate card risk and 3DS posture. This platform automates BIN ingestion, enrichment, classification, and reporting with a dashboard and API, enabling faster decisions and easier integration into fraud workflows.
