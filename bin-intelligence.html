Investor Summary

Fraud prevention teams need reliable BIN intelligence to evaluate card risk and 3DS posture. This platform automates BIN ingestion, enrichment, classification, and reporting with a dashboard and API, enabling faster decisions and easier integration into fraud workflows.

E-commerce fraud costs merchants billions annually. Our platform provides the intelligence layer that fraud teams need — real-time BIN data enrichment, exploit classification, and risk scoring that integrates seamlessly into existing transaction processing workflows.
