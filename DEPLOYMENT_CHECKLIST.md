## Final Verification Before Launch

### The Ultimate Pre-Launch Test:
1. Open site on your phone
2. Navigate to investor section
3. Try to submit contact form
4. Check if you receive the email
5. Load site on 4G (not WiFi)
6. Show to someone unfamiliar - can they understand what you do in 10 seconds?

**If all above work perfectly, you're ready to launch!**
