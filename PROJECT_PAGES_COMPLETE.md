4. **Features Section** - 6-card grid (3+3 symmetrical layout)
5. **Technology Section** - 2-column grid (technical details + architecture)
6. **Use Cases Section** - 3-card grid (symmetrical layout)

8. **CTA Section** - Centered call-to-action with dual buttons
