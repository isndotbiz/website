Investor Summary

Video content demand is massive, but production is expensive and slow. This system automates the core workflow from content ingestion through scripts, visuals, narration, assembly, and upload — enabling a scalable content operation with consistent output quality.

The creator economy has exploded to $12B, yet most creators face the same bottleneck: producing quality video content is time-consuming and costly. Our pipeline transforms a single URL into a fully-rendered, narrated video ready for upload — in minutes, not days.
