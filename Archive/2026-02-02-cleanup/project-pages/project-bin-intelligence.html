Project Overview

Enabling Faster, Smarter Fraud Prevention

Fraud prevention teams need reliable BIN intelligence to evaluate card risk and 3DS posture, but traditional solutions are fragmented and slow. This platform automates BIN ingestion, enrichment, classification, and reporting with both a dashboard and API, enabling faster fraud decisions and easier integration into existing workflows.

Business Value

By providing automated BIN intelligence with exploit classification in a single unified system, we eliminate manual data gathering and analysis bottlenecks. Fraud teams can make real-time risk assessments while security analytics teams gain actionable threat insights, reducing fraud losses and improving operational efficiency for e-commerce platforms and payment processors.
