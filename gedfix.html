Core Features

Tree Visualization

Pedigree charts, fan charts, descendant trees, timeline views, and interactive maps with geocoded events.

AI Research

Automated research proposals from 7 AI providers. Source finding across FamilySearch and Chronicling America. Narrative generation.

Source Validation

Auto-classify sources as online trees vs. primary records. Flag unvalidated links. GPS checklist tracking per person.

Source Validation Engine

Every link in your tree needs a source other than an online tree to be considered validated. GedFix automatically classifies sources by type and tracks validation progress across your entire tree.
