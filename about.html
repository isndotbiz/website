Our Origin

Where Innovation Meets Execution

In 2015, we saw a gap in the market: businesses struggling to harness emerging AI and cloud technologies for real competitive advantage. Most software companies were either pure product vendors or traditional consultancies. Neither model delivered what enterprises truly needed.

We founded iSN.BiZ Inc with a different vision: become the technical partner that combines deep engineering expertise with strategic business thinking. Not just building software, but architecting solutions that transform how organizations operate.

Our approach was contrarian from day one. While others chased every new framework, we focused on fundamental engineering principles and measurable business outcomes. While competitors sold cloud services, we built expertise in self-hosted AI infrastructure for data sovereignty. While the industry talked about AI, we shipped production systems.

Today, our 100% client retention rate and portfolio of transformational projects validate this approach. We've proven that exceptional technical execution, combined with unwavering focus on business value, creates lasting partnerships and sustainable growth.
