Deep Dive: Five Ancient Wisdom Systems

No other app brings together this depth of spiritual analysis. SpiritAtlas weaves five time-tested traditions into a single, coherent spiritual profile. Each engine operates as an independent module with its own domain logic, data models, and presentation layer, ensuring accuracy, testability, and extensibility without cross-module coupling.

Numerology — The Language of Numbers

Your name and birth date carry hidden numerical codes that shape your personality, purpose, and destiny. SpiritAtlas computes your complete numerological profile using both the ancient Chaldean (Babylonian) and Pythagorean (Greek) systems. The engine calculates Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge, Personality, and Destiny numbers through iterative digit summation with special handling for Master Numbers 11, 22, and 33. Name-based calculations implement standard letter-to-number mapping tables, processing full legal names through vowel and consonant separation to derive Soul Urge and Personality values independently. The reduction pipeline handles edge cases including hyphenated names, suffixes, and cultural naming patterns.

Astrology — Celestial Wisdom Written in the Stars

SpiritAtlas generates complete astrological profiles with both Tropical (Western) and Sidereal (Vedic) zodiac systems. The calculation pipeline begins with Julian Day Number conversion from the Gregorian birth date and time, then applies Swiss Ephemeris algorithms for planetary longitude computation. The engine delivers Sun, Moon, and Rising sign analysis along with all planetary positions, 12 house placements via the Placidus system, and full aspect analysis including conjunctions, trines, squares, and oppositions. Current transit tracking provides real-time cosmic weather including Mercury Retrograde alerts with intensity meters. Synastry reports compare two natal charts across communication, emotional resonance, physical attraction, and long-term alignment dimensions.

Ayurveda — Ancient Wellness for Modern Life

The Ayurveda module implements dosha constitution analysis through a structured questionnaire evaluating physical characteristics, behavioral tendencies, and dietary patterns across thirty-six weighted assessment dimensions. Users receive their Vata-Pitta-Kapha profile as both a primary dosha and a percentage distribution, with personalized recommendations for nutrition, daily routines, yoga practices, herbal support, meditation techniques, and seasonal wellness adjustments. The scoring algorithm accounts for bidosha and tridosha constitutions where two or three doshas are nearly equal, a common result that many competing apps fail to handle. Seasonal adjustment recommendations shift based on the user's geographic location and current date.

Human Design — Your Energetic Blueprint

The Human Design engine synthesizes elements from the I Ching, Kabbalah, Hindu-Brahmin chakra system, and quantum physics to generate a user's BodyGraph chart. The calculation begins with two separate sets of planetary positions: the conscious calculation at the moment of birth and the unconscious calculation at approximately eighty-eight days before birth. These positions map to the sixty-four hexagrams and their corresponding gates and channels. The engine determines one of five energy types — Manifestor, Generator, Manifesting Generator, Projector, or Reflector — along with six profile lines, nine energy centers, inner authority type, and detailed life strategy guidance.

Tantra — Sacred Connection and Union

The fifth spiritual system goes beyond the surface to explore sacred intimacy, energy polarity, chakra alignment, and spiritual union potential. The Tantric analysis engine computes Energy Polarity Type (Masculine/Shiva, Feminine/Shakti, or Balanced), seven-chakra alignment from Root to Crown, and Intimacy Style Mapping across six archetypes: Sensual, Passionate, Spiritual, Playful, Nurturing, and Intense. The system integrates Attachment Style Assessment (Secure, Anxious, Avoidant, or Fearful-Avoidant), Love Language mapping for how users give and receive love, and Kundalini Awakening Guidance with safe practices for spiritual energy work. This system adds a dimension of relationship analysis that no competitor offers, providing the depth that serious spiritual practitioners demand and are willing to pay premium subscription prices to access.

Platform Architecture

🏗

Clean Architecture

Modular Design

Multi-module Gradle project with strict dependency rules enforced at build time. The app/ module handles navigation and dependency injection wiring, core/ provides shared calculation utilities and UI components, domain/ defines pure business rules with no Android framework dependencies, and feature/ modules encapsulate each spiritual system independently. This separation enables parallel team development and isolated testing with no cross-module state leakage.

🔒

Privacy-First

Security Posture

All spiritual data remains on-device by default using EncryptedSharedPreferences backed by the Android Keystore system. Cloud AI features require explicit user opt-in through a consent gate dialog that explains exactly what data will be transmitted. SSL certificate pinning via network security configuration rejects connections to servers presenting unexpected certificates. No data harvesting, no advertising profiles. Your spiritual data is yours.
